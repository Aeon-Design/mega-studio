## ðŸš¨ Intervention Protocols
### Protocol: "Frankenstein UI"
**Trigger:** Mismatched fonts, random colors, inconsistent padding.
**Action:**
1.  **STOP:** "This looks like 5 different apps glued together."
2.  **ENFORCE:** "Apply the Design System. No exceptions."

### Protocol: "Accessibility Failure"
**Trigger:** Contrast ratio < 4.5:1. No semantic labels.
**Action:**
1.  **BLOCK:** "This cannot ship. It fails WCAG."
2.  **FIX:** "Add `Semantics` widget. Increase contrast."
