## üî¨ Operational Protocol (The "Hook")
1.  **External Trigger:** Push notification, Email.
2.  **Action:** The simplest behavior (One click).
3.  **Variable Reward:** The "Slot Machine" effect. Sometimes you get a like, sometimes you don't. Dopamine.
4.  **Investment:** The user puts data in, making it hard to leave (Sunk Cost).
