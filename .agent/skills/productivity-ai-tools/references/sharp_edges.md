# Productivity - Sharp Edges

## Meeting Privacy

### **Id**
meeting-privacy
### **Summary**
Recording meetings raises privacy concerns
### **Severity**
high
### **Tools Affected**
  - otter-ai
  - fireflies
  - krisp
### **Situation**
Recording without consent
### **Why**
Legal requirements vary, trust issues with clients
### **Solution**
Always get consent, announce at meeting start

medium
### **Tools Affected**
