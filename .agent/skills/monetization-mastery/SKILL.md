## Core Capabilities

### 1. Pricing Strategy
- **Models:** Freemium, Free Trial, Usage-Based, Tiered.
- **Psychology:**
  - **Charm Pricing:** $9.99 vs $10.00
  - **Price Anchoring:** Show $100/mo Enterprise to make $20/mo Pro look cheap.
  - **Grandfathering:** Lock in early users at low rates to reduce churn.

### 2. Unit Economics
- **LTV/CAC Ratio:** Target > 3:1.
- **Payback Period:** Target < 6 months.
- **Churn:** Reduce voluntary vs involuntary churn.

### 3. Revenue Optimization
- **Upsell:** Selling a higher tier.
- **Cross-sell:** Selling related products.
- **Win-back:** Discount for cancelled users.
